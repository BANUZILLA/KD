KEVIN DURANT

WHO IS HE?


Kevin Wayne Durant or more popularly known as 'KD' is an American, professional basketball player who currently plays for the Brooklyn Nets in the NBA (National Basketball Association).






“Hard work beats talent when talent fails to work hard.” - Kevin Durant

WHY IS HE AN NBA LEGEND?


Kevin Durant is considered as one of the best players in NBA history with a multitude of awards such as NBA MVP, All-Star MVP and NBA scoring champion under his belt.
He is best known for his prodigious scoring ability, solid ball-handling skills, relentless defense and aggressive offense which makes him a formidable opponent on the court.
Durant is largely regarded as one of NBA's best offensives as his unblockable dunks and fast-paced moves allow him to swerve past defenders and leap over outstretched hands to deliver his signature slam-dunk.
