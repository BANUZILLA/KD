Things You Didn't Know

You've Got A Friend In Me

During his early career playing in the Amateur Athletic Union, Durant was teammates with future NBA player Michael Beasley, who he is still friends with to this day.

Number 35

All the way up to playing with the Brooklyn Nets, Durant has worn the number 35 on his jersey in honour of his former AAU coach, Charles Craig who was murdered at age 35.

Bigfoot

Kevin Durant has unusually large and long feet and wears size 18 shoes to compensate for them. In fact, his unique feet have often been described as 'hockey sticks' by fans.

Olympic Gold

In the Rio de Janeiro Olympic Games, Kevin Durant won gold for team USA in basketball, scoring 30 out of their 96 points against Serbia and bringing home the gold medal for USA.

King of H-O-R-S-E

On the rare occasions when NBA decided to host a H-O-R-S-E competition, Kevin Durant showed-off his skills by winning the 2008-09 and 2009-10 competitions.

Bon Appétit

Yes, it's true. Kevin Durant has his own restaurant and its called “Kd’s Southern Cuisine.” His restaurant serves a variety of foods from prime beef to tenderloin medallions.

Human Pterodactyl

Kevin Durant has long arms, really long arms. His total wingspan equals to 2.25 metres long which is longer than he is tall and allows him to block nearly any shot.

Old Habits

Durant was offered a $70 mllion contract from Adidas, however he declined this in favour of a smaller contract with Nike as he had worn Nikes as a kid.
